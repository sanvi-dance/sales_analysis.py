# 📊 Sales Data Analysis Report

## Objective
To analyze sales data and extract meaningful insights using Python and pandas.

---

## Data Cleaning
- Removed duplicate records
- Handled missing values:
  - Numerical columns filled with mean
  - Categorical columns filled with mode

---

## Key Metrics
- **Total Revenue:** Calculated using sum of sales
- **Average Revenue:** Mean of total sales
- **Best Selling Product:** Product with highest total revenue
- **Revenue by Region:** Aggregated sales across regions

---

## Insights
- The product **Laptop** generates the highest revenue.
- Revenue distribution varies across regions.
- High-performing regions and products should be prioritized.
- Improving low-performing products can increase overall revenue.

---

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib (optional)

---

## Conclusion
This analysis helps identify top-performing products and regions, enabling better business decision-making.
